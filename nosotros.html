About us

WHO ARE WE?

We are a passionate group of innovators committed to creating a sustainable future. Our mission with this project is to raise awareness among youth and adults about the impact of everyday decisions on the environment through interactive and educational experiences. Since the beginning of our project, we have believed that education and technology are powerful tools to empower people. Through our video game, ✨ KEPLER-16B, we seek to provide a space where players can explore, experience and learn about the interconnectedness of earth systems and the importance of making responsible decisions.

Game Narrative

Through the games, players experience situations that demonstrate the interconnectedness of earth systems and how poor daily decisions, both small and large scale, can lead to natural disasters. Using satellite data provided by NASA, the game allows for various predictions that feed back into each level, bringing players closer and closer to a sustainable future.
